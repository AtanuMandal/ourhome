Cost Optimisation Recommendations
📩

Replace ACS SMS with Indian Provider

Switch OTP and notification SMS from ACS ($0.04/msg) to MSG91 or Textlocal ($0.003–0.005/msg). Saves up to 90% of SMS costs — the single biggest saving available.

🔔

Use Push Notifications over SMS

Visitor alerts, notice notifications, and complaint updates are already sent via browser push (VAPID — free). Avoid SMS for these; reserve SMS for OTP only.

🔍

Tune Cosmos DB Indexes

Each unnecessary index increases write RU consumption. Audit index policy per container — favour composite indexes on (societyId, status, date) and exclude unused fields.

🌐

Switch APIM to Standard v2 at Scale

At the Scale tier with 5.74M calls, Consumption APIM costs ~$166/mo. APIM Standard v2 ($250/mo flat) includes 2.5M calls + $0.0035/1K beyond — evaluate break-even at ~8M calls/month.

🗄️

Archive Old Blob Data

Visitor photos and payment proofs older than 12 months can be moved to Blob Archive tier ($0.002/GB vs $0.018/GB hot). Reduces Blob costs by 85–90% for aged data.

📊

Cap App Insights Ingestion

Set a daily data cap on App Insights to avoid surprise costs. Use sampling (already configured in host.json) and exclude noisy health-probe logs from ingestion.

Important Notes
  • Estimates only: Actual costs depend on real usage patterns, Azure region pricing, and promotional discounts. Use the Azure Pricing Calculator for live quotes.
  • Region: Prices are based on the Central India Azure region. Costs may vary by 5–20% in other regions.
  • Azure Free Tier: New Azure accounts have a 12-month free tier and $200 credit; Starter-tier costs may be $0 in the first months.
  • No Reserved Capacity: These estimates use pay-as-you-go / Serverless pricing. Reserved capacity (for Cosmos DB Provisioned Throughput) could reduce DB costs by 30–60% at predictable scale but requires upfront commitment.
  • Support Plan not included: Azure support plans ($29–$300/mo) are not part of this estimate.
  • Payment gateway not included: Razorpay / Stripe payment processing fees (0.5–2% per transaction) are not included — these are future-phase costs from recurring_fee_payment.md.
