# Account / Fee Management

## Overview
The account and fee management module will allow owners to manage their monthly maintenance fees and other charges. It will also provide society management with tools to track and manage the society's financials, including expenses, balance sheets, collections, income, and expenses.

## Features

1. **Payment Management**
   - Owners can view their monthly maintenance fees and other charges.
   - Online payment gateway integration for seamless fee payments.
   - Payment history and receipts available for download.

2. **Society Account Details**
   - View detailed account statements for the society.
   - Breakdown of income and expenses.
   - Monthly and yearly financial reports.

3. **Expense Management**
   - Track and categorize society expenses.
   - Generate expense reports.

4. **Balance Sheet**
   - Generate and view the society's balance sheet.
   - Compare income vs. expenses.

5. **Notifications**
   - Notify owners about upcoming due dates for payments.
   - Alerts for overdue payments.

6. **Admin Features**
   - Add, edit, and delete fee structures.
   - Approve or reject payments.
   - Generate custom financial reports.
