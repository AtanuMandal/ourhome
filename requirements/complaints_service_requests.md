# Complaints and Service Requests

## Overview
This module will allow owners and residents to raise complaints and service requests to the society management. It will also provide tools for tracking and resolving these requests.

## Features

1. **Raise Complaints**
   - Residents can submit complaints with details and attachments (e.g., photos).
   - Categorize complaints (e.g., maintenance, security, noise).

2. **Service Requests**
   - Residents can request services (e.g., plumbing, electrical repairs).
   - Option to select preferred time slots for service.

3. **Complaint/Request Tracking**
   - Residents can track the status of their complaints and requests.
   - Notifications for status updates (e.g., in-progress, resolved).

4. **Admin Features**
   - Assign complaints and requests to relevant personnel.
   - Track resolution times and generate reports.

5. **Feedback and Ratings**
   - Residents can provide feedback and rate the resolution of their complaints or service requests.
