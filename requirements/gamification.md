# Gamification

## Overview
The gamification module will introduce inter-apartment competitions and activities to encourage community engagement and participation. This will make the society more interactive and fun for residents.

## Features

1. **Competitions and Challenges**
   - Organize inter-apartment competitions (e.g., sports, quizzes, cultural events).
   - Track scores and leaderboards.

2. **Rewards System**
   - Reward residents with points for participation and winning competitions.
   - Redeem points for benefits (e.g., discounts on maintenance fees, access to premium amenities).

3. **Event Management**
   - Create and manage events for the society.
   - Allow residents to register for events.

4. **Community Engagement**
   - Encourage residents to participate in community activities.
   - Share updates and results of competitions.

5. **Notifications**
   - Notify residents about upcoming competitions and events.
   - Send reminders for event participation.

6. **Admin Features**
   - Create and manage competitions and events.
   - Monitor participation and engagement.
   - Generate reports on community activities.
