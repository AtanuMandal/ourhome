# Local Service Providers

## Overview
This module will allow local service providers (e.g., grocery stores, cab services, plumbers) to view service requests posted by individual residents and offer their services. This will create a marketplace for local services within the society.

## Features

1. **Service Provider Registration**
   - Local service providers can register on the platform.
   - Provide details about their services, pricing, and contact information.

2. **Service Requests**
   - View service requests posted by residents.
   - Option to accept or decline service requests.

3. **Resident Features**
   - Residents can browse registered service providers.
   - Post service requests with details and preferred time slots.
   - Rate and review service providers after service completion.

4. **Notifications**
   - Notify service providers about new service requests.
   - Notify residents when a service provider accepts their request.

5. **Admin Features**
   - Approve or reject service provider registrations.
   - Monitor service requests and responses.
   - Generate reports on service provider activities.
