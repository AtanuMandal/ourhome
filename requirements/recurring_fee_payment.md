# Recurring Fee Payment

## Overview
The recurring fee payment module will allow residents to set up automatic payments for their monthly maintenance fees and other recurring charges. This will ensure timely payments and reduce manual effort.

## Features

1. **Setup Recurring Payments**
   - Residents can set up recurring payments for maintenance fees and other charges.
   - Option to select payment frequency (e.g., monthly, quarterly).

2. **Payment Methods**
   - Support for multiple payment methods (e.g., credit card, debit card, net banking).
   - Secure storage of payment details.

3. **Payment Reminders**
   - Notify residents before the payment is deducted.
   - Option to modify or cancel recurring payments.

4. **Payment History**
   - View history of all recurring payments.
   - Download receipts for recurring payments.

5. **Admin Features**
   - View and manage residents' recurring payment setups.
   - Generate reports on recurring payments.
